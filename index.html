Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Party Games, developed and published by Stegosaurini (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

In short: Party Games is an online mobile party game. The app currently does not require users to create an account and we do not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, contacts, photos, or precise location. However, the app includes advertising and in-app purchases, which may involve third-party services that process certain device, advertising, purchase, usage, or diagnostic data as described below.

1. Information we do not directly collect

We do not currently ask users to create an account and we do not directly collect or store personal information such as:

  • name or surname;
  • email address;
  • phone number;
  • contacts;
  • photos or videos from the device;
  • precise GPS location;
  • user-generated account profile information.

2. Online features and technical data

Party Games is an online app. When the app connects to online services, certain technical information may be processed automatically to provide, secure, and improve the service. This may include basic device and connection information, such as device type, operating system, app version, approximate region derived from network data, and technical logs.

3. Advertising

Party Games displays advertisements. Advertising partners may collect or process data to deliver ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations. Depending on the advertising service used, this data may include:

  • advertising identifiers, such as the Android Advertising ID;
  • device information, such as device model, operating system, language, and app version;
  • approximate location derived from IP address;
  • ad interactions, such as impressions, clicks, and views;
  • diagnostic and performance information related to ad delivery.

These advertising services may use this information to show contextual or personalized ads, depending on user settings, consent choices, platform settings, age settings, and applicable law.

4. In-app purchases

Party Games offers in-app purchases. Payments and purchase processing are handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store full payment card details. We may receive limited purchase-related information necessary to unlock purchased content, verify purchases, prevent fraud, and provide support. This may include transaction identifiers, purchased item identifiers, purchase status, and related technical information.

5. Local data stored on your device

The app may store certain information locally on your device, such as game settings, preferences, unlock status, or gameplay-related progress. This information is used to make the app work properly and improve the user experience. Local data may be deleted if you uninstall the app or clear the app’s data from your device settings.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share or make data available only in the following cases:

  • with service providers and third-party SDKs that help us operate the app, such as advertising and payment services;
  • when required to comply with legal obligations;
  • to protect the security, integrity, and rights of our app, users, and business;
  • in connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition, if applicable in the future.

7. Children’s privacy

Party Games is intended for a general audience unless stated otherwise on the app store listing. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information where required.

8. User choices and controls

You can control certain privacy settings directly from your device or Google account, including ad personalization settings and permissions granted to the app. You can also reset or delete your Android Advertising ID through your device settings where supported.

9. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to their own policies and legal obligations.

10. Future features

We may add new features in the future, such as accounts, online profiles, leaderboards, cloud saves, analytics, or additional social features. If these features involve additional data collection or processing, we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required, request appropriate consent or provide additional disclosures.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are published means that the updated policy applies.
